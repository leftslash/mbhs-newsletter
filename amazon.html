Please remember while shopping... Make Your Everyday Shopping Count
Do you order from Amazon?
If so you can donate to the MBHS PA at the same time without a single extra click and without spending any additional money!
Setting up for the first time
Login to Amazon Smile. When prompted to select a charity, type "Millennium Brooklyn" in the search box and select "Parent Association of Millennium Brooklyn High School"  as your charity with Amazon. Then you can start shopping and contributing directly to Parent Association of MBHS.
What if I already have a charity set up?
Use the drop down menu next to your designated charity, located directly under the search bar at the top of the screen and search for Parent Association of Millennium Brooklyn High School.  Then set it as your default charity.
What do I do the next time I want to shop?
Be sure you go to the https://smile.amazon.com/?tag=mbhspa-20 - this will always automatically redirect all your purchases to the Parent Association of Millennium Brooklyn High School.
