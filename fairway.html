Fairway Community Partnership Cards
If you did not receive a Fairway card and would like one please send an email to the PA and we will get you one ASAP! Families can use the card with each purchase made at Fairway stores and Fairway will give MBHS PA 3% of each purchase in cash!! This is an easy way to support the PA and it doesn't cost the families anything. All you have to do is remember to give your card to the cashier when you shop at Fairway.
